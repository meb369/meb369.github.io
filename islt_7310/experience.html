School District of Washington

Washington, MO
2008 - Present
Director of Orchestras

As Director of Orchestras, I oversee 150+ students annually enrolled in orchestra in grades 5-12. Four levels of orchestras perform 7 concerts each year. During my tenure, we have participated in 4 national competitions, achieving Gold and Silver ratings each time. Additionally, our high school orchestra has achieved the highest rating possible for our State Large Ensemble 8 of my 12 years in Washington.

Private Violin and Viola Studio

Washington, MO
2009-Present
Private Instructor

On the completion of my first year in Washington, I developed a private violin and viola studio out of my home during the summer. Due to our distance from major metropolitan area, many students in our area did not have access to private instruction on their instruments through the summer months. Also, since I am the director of orchestras in the local district, my studio routinely has between 20-25 students weekly, with continuity from their in-school curriculum. Beyond their curriculum, students who participate in summer lessons also seek out guidance for out-of-school performance opportunities such as weddings and corporate events.

Gateway Arch

St. Louis, MO
2004-2008
Tour Guide

While this position does not directly connect to my career as an educator, it was integral in my development of presentation skills. My role as tour guide was to inform Gateway Arch patrons how to safely enjoy what the tram and viewing platform had to offer. Daily I interacted with 5,000 patrons in an organizational, social, and informational role.
